Welcome to the hydro3D project

hydro3D is a finite difference Navier Stokes solver that permits accurate and efficient Large Eddy Simulation (LES) of turbulent flows. It has been developed by Prof. Thorsten Stoesser and co-workers at Cardiff University, UK, primarily for use in the simulation of environmental fluid dynamics. If you are interested in using and/or developing the code, or if you would like to discuss opportunities for collaboration, please send a mail to mcsherryr@cardiff.ac.uk. To get a feel for the code's present range of capabilities please take a look at our flow gallery and publications.

Features

  • Sub-grid Scale (SGS) turbulent stress modelling using Smagorinsky or WALE methods.
  • Choice of spatial discretisation schemes including 4th order central differences.
  • Choice of temporal solution schemes including 5th order ENO/WENO.
  • Efficient computation of flows in and over complex geometries using the Immersed Boundary Method.
  • Efficient multigrid solver with MPI and OpenMP parallelisation.
  • Local mesh refinement for increased efficiency and accuracy.
  • Bubble column simulation using Immersed Boundary particle tracking.

Ongoing development

  • 1-equation SGS modelling.
  • Free surface simulation in turbulent flows using the Level Set Method.
  • Sediment and scalar transport.
